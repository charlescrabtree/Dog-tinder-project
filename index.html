Welcome to Barkle!

Here is a guide on how Barkle works!

Welcome to Barkle! Thank you for joining us on the #1 platform for puppers to make new friends! We encourage everyone to take a look around and search through the mass amount of users to see who fits your puppers the best! By using the sidebar navigation, users can easily toggle between the different pages of Barkle. The home page is where users can find a brief description of Barkle and how it works. The pawfile page is where users can update their information and add a profile picture. The dogpark page is where users can search through all the users of Barkle and find the perfect match for their puppers. The chat page is where users can chat with other users and make new friends. The doghouse page is where users can save their favorite users and keep track of them. The creators page is where users can find information about the creators of Barkle. We wish you a merry time on Barkle and hope you find the perfect match for your puppers!
